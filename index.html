How It Works

  1. Play games on Rugs.Fun — every wager you place adds to your monthly total.
  2. The leaderboard auto-sorts by total wager (highest first).
  3. Top players share the monthly prize (1 SOL) — resets on the 1st of each month (PST).
  4. Update leaderboard.json in the repo to change scores.

FAQ

What is Wagerboard?
A public monthly leaderboard showing top wagerers on Rugs.Fun. Top players earn rewards.
When does it reset?
The leaderboard resets at 12:00 AM (midnight) PST on the 1st of each month.
How do I update my score?
For now, scores are updated by editing leaderboard.json in this repository and committing the change.
